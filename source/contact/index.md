## Submit Content
If you have a recipe, tool teardown, or evasion lab you want to share:

1. Email a short outline (goal, tooling, detection notes) to `contact@redteamrecipes.com`.
2. Include screenshots or PoC snippets so we can reproduce the findings.
3. Tell us how you want to be credited (handle, team, or anonymous).

We collaborate directly with authors to review, test, and publish each piece under the RTR banner.
